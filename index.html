Jinglin Xu

Currently, I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jiwen Lu. I received my Ph.D. degree at Northwestern Polytechnical University under the supervision of Prof. Junwei Han.

My research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning. Currently, I am working in the fields of video understanding and vision-language understanding.
