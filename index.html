Jinglin Xu

I am now an Associate Professor in the School of Intelligence Science and Technology at the University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB), a council member and deputy secretary-general of the Beijing Society of Image and Graphics (BSIG). Before joining USTB, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University. I received my Ph.D. degree at Northwestern Polytechnical University. I was selected for the 9th Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program by CAST (2023) and won the Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award of CSIG in 2022 (with only ten winners per year) and the First Prize of Natural Science Award of CAA in 2023 (4/5).

My research interests lie in video action understanding, multimodal fine-grained analysis and generation, and 3D human pose estimation.

We actively recruit Post-doc Fellows, Ph.D./Master students, and Research Interns! Welcome to contact me with your detailed CV!
